BRITTLE

Mental Health Campaign

A project that plays heavily on the idea of a strong signifier. For this I use a Brick as a metaphor for masculinity and play on this object yet being strong it still is fairly fragile and brittle. Themed around construction with my choice of large stencilled type.

I produced posters, leaflets, printed merchandise, a website design and some pieces of moving image for social media platforms.
