Dimension Typeface

Experimental Typography

Produced as a means to further understand type design.
A architectural typeface that relies heavily on negative space, with a juxtaposition of styles and identity. A combination of fine and heavy strokes reminiscent of a Didot typeface yet at heavier width it feels like a homage to 80s science fiction.


I generated various morphed and distorted photogram posters using the type.
